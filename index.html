Challenges & Constructive Measures

1. High Initial Capital cost

Cons: Developing smart infrastructure and renewable systems demands heavy upfront investment.
Counter measure: Adopt phased PPP projects & land-value-capture financing to spread costs & ensure long-term returns.

2. Renewable Reliability

Cons: Solar and wind energy supply may fluctuate seasonally and may affect consistency.
Constructive measure: Use hybrid energy grids with battery & hydrogen storage for stable, 24/7 renewable power.

3. Population Growth & Urban sprawl

Cons: Rapid migration could strain housing & transport systems.
Constructive measure: Promote vertical, mixed-use zoning & green buffer zones to maintain sustainable density.
